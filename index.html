Remittances Remain Near $3 Billion Level for Ninth Straight Month in November

Remittances sent by overseas Pakistanis increased by 29 percent to $2.915 billion in November 2024 compared to inflows of $2.259 billion reported in the same month of last fiscal year (FY24).
